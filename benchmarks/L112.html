Datasheet Description:

16.0 mi W from Tucson. 6.2 miles west of the U. S. Veterans Hospital, Tucson, along state highway 86 (Ajo-Tucson), thence 9.8 miles northwest along the Kinney road (via McCain Loop), 0.25 mile west of a rock culvert under the road, 0.2 mile east of a point where the road crosses a canyon, 22 1/2 feet north of the center line of the road, 1.9 feet south of a witness post, and set in the top of a concrete post projecting 0.9 foot above the ground, and about 1 foot higher than the road.

"L 112" is a benchmark monumented by the USC&GS in 1935 located in Coconino County, Arizona.

The mark is on the north side of Kinney Road. There is a parking pullout southeasteast of the benchmark.
