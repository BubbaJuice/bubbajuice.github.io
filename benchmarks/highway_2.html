Datasheet Description:

RM 2--standard RM tablet stamped---HIGHWAY NO 2 1946---, set in rock.

"HIGHWAY NO 2" is a benchmark monumented by the USGS in 1946 located in Pinal County, Arizona. It is a deticated reference mark for HIGHWAY.

The best way to get to "HIGHWAY NO 2" is to drive north on old State Route 77 from State Route 77 for half a mile. This road is passable with most-all passenger vehicles. From here, walk on the power line road to ridge. Pack up the ridge to top of hill. The reference mark #1 is on the ridge under a tree just before the summit.

"HIGHWAY NO 2" is located 25.820 feet or 7.870 meters 265 degrees from the main benchmark. It is located above the station on a boulder.
