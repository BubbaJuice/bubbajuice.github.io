Alright this looks cool and all, but how does one find a benchmark?

A good place to start for beginners is to look on https://geodesy.noaa.gov/NGSDataExplorer/, the NGS benchmark map. To use this map, scroll to the place where you would like to search for benchmarks. Then, on the far left click on the "Map Layers" tab. Press "Find Marks" and benchmarks will appear on your screen. Click on any benchmark and press "Datasheet". tbc...
